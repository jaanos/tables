D. M. Mesner

The main purpose of this page is to make Dale Mesner's 1956 Ph.D. thesis available, since there do not seem to exist public copies. The present copy was made by Misha Klin. This thesis is of historical interest since it contains the first construction of the graph that is now known as the Higman-Sims graph. We add a few more items.

Data

Dale Marsh Mesner was born on 1923-04-13, defended his Ph.D. thesis on 1956-05-22, and died 2009-12-08, at the age of 87.
