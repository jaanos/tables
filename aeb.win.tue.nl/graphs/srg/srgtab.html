Parameters of Strongly Regular Graphs

Below tables with parameters for strongly regular graphs.

The columns are:

  • existence
  • v - number of vertices
  • k - valency
  • λ - number of common neighbours of two adjacent vertices
  • μ - number of common neighbours of two nonadjacent vertices
  • rf - positive eigenvalue with multiplicity
  • sg - negative eigenvalue with multiplicity
  • comments

The comments are undocumented at present. Ask.
